Peregrine Falcon

Below is a picture of this falcon. Click on the picture to learn more.

Below are some more interesting facts about the peregrine falcon:

  1. The peregrine falcon is seen as one of the fastest birds in the world, with speeds of approximately 200 mph when diving for prey.
  2. This bird often does not build a nest, choosing instead to raise its young on flat surfaces like cliffs or skyscraper window ledges.
  3. The bird almost went extinct due to the use of the pesticide DDT, but has since made a comeback.
